What We Do?

The service we offer is specifically designed to meet your needs.

  • Support Coordination

    We offer a comprehensive Support Coordination to help you navigate the complexities of your NDIS plan.

  • Community Participation

    We encourage engagement within the community to develop a sense of velonging and contribution.

  • In-House Support

    Our support includes assistance with household chores, meal preparation, and maintaining a safe living environment.

  • Health and Wellbeing Program

    We provide opportunities for participants to be involved in various health and wellbeing activities.

Why Choose Us?

Tailored to you

  • We specialise in providing recovery-focused support to individuals aged 18-64 living with disabilities and special needs. Our approach is designed to empower and uplift our participants, guiding them toward achieving personal recovery goals.

  • Our Services are clien-centere, focusing on the individual's needs and goals.We work with a holistic approach, considering all aspects of a person's life to help them achieve healthy and purposeful lives.

  • Many individuals face challenges forming community-based connections with service providers and social networks, leading to social isolation and impacting their psychological well-being. We are dedicated to combating this issue by fostering strong, supportive community connections.

  • We have experienced NDIS support coordinator. We can help you navigate the multifaceted world of NDIS plan management.

About US

At the heart of our operations, we provide person-centred disability support services to NDIS participants across Geelong, Barwon, Surf Coast, and the Western Suburbs.

  • Vision

    We aspire to a world where inclusivity and respect are universal, ensuring that every person with a disability is empowered to achieve their highest potential.

  • Mission

    Our mission is to deliver exceptional, person-centred care and advocacy for people with disabilities, cultivating a community where everyone is empowered to succeed and flourish.

  • Purpose

    Our purpose is to transform the lives of individuals with disabilities by championing unity, support, and societal change towards genuine inclusion and equality.

  • Values

    • Empowerment
    • Advocacy
    • Community
    • Quality Care
